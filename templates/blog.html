Architecture Snapshot

The inference stack couples a symptom normalization layer with a binary vectorizer feeding a Support Vector Classifier. Enrichment joins pull structured guidance— description, precautions, diet, workouts and medication references— from CSV data tables loaded at startup. This keeps latency low for repeated requests.

  • Normalization: remove punctuation, lower case, convert spaces to underscores.
  • Model: SVC (pickle) with feature index mapping dictionary.
  • Planned: multi-condition scoring, exposure of feature influence signals.

Roadmap

  1. Confidence tiers & disclaimers alignment.
  2. Synonym expansion / fuzzy matching.
  3. Lightweight metrics dashboard.
  4. Versioned model banner metadata.

Ethics & Safety

We frame predictions as possibilities, not diagnoses. No storage of personal identifiers. UI reinforces escalation to professional care when symptoms are severe or persistent.
