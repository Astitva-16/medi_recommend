Our Mission

SymptoCare accelerates the journey from uncertainty to understanding by transforming raw symptom phrases into structured clinical context. We combine ML pattern recognition with curated supportive guidance (precautions, diet, exercise, medication references) to help users prepare better conversations with professionals.

We deliberately avoid alarm language and always reinforce that medical assessment requires qualified practitioners.

Principles

  • Clarity: Plain language outputs.
  • Context: Surround predictions with lifestyle & precaution hints.
  • Non‑diagnostic: Present probabilities, not certainties.
  • Privacy‑lean: No persistence layer in this demo build.

Pipeline

  1. Normalize user symptom string.
  2. Vectorize into binary feature map.
  3. Model inference (SVC index → disease label).
  4. Lookup enrichment tables for description & recommendations.

Disclaimer

Outputs may be incomplete or inaccurate. Consult a licensed professional for diagnosis and treatment decisions.

Planned upgrades: symptom synonym expansion, multi‑label scoring, feature attribution surfaces.
