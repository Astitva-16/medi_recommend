Frequently Asked Questions

Is HealthCare Assistant a replacement for professional medical advice?

No, HealthCare Assistant is designed to provide informational support and preliminary guidance. It should never replace professional medical consultation, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals for medical concerns.

How accurate are the predictions?

Our machine learning models are trained on extensive datasets and provide reliable preliminary assessments. However, accuracy can vary based on symptom complexity and individual health factors. Results should be used as guidance for further medical consultation.

Is my health information secure?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy and data security. All information is processed securely, and we follow industry best practices for data protection. We do not store personal health information beyond the session.

How can I provide feedback or suggestions?

We welcome your feedback! You can use the contact form above, email us directly, or reach out through our support channels. Your input helps us improve the platform for everyone.
